Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

MarkdownToRichText ("we", "our", or "the add-on") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Google Sheets add-on.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or spreadsheet content.

The add-on operates entirely within your Google Sheets environment. All text processing happens locally in your browser, and no data is sent to external servers.

How the Add-on Works

MarkdownToRichText:

  • Reads the content of selected cells in your Google Sheets document
  • Converts Markdown syntax to rich text formatting
  • Writes the formatted text back to the same cells

All of these operations occur within Google's infrastructure and your browser. No data leaves the Google ecosystem.

Google API Scopes

The add-on requires the following permissions:

  • View and manage spreadsheets - Required to read Markdown text and apply rich text formatting to cells
  • Display UI elements - Required to show menus and dialogs within Google Sheets

These permissions are used solely for the functionality of converting Markdown to rich text. We do not access any other data in your Google account.

Data Storage

We do not store any data. The add-on does not have a backend server or database. Your spreadsheet content remains within Google's servers and is subject to Google's privacy practices.

Third-Party Services

The add-on does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services. It operates exclusively within the Google Workspace environment.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any data, there is no risk of data breach from our add-on. Your data remains protected by Google's security measures.

Children's Privacy

The add-on does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
